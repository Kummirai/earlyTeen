Dear early teenagers, you are at a pivotal stage of life, a time of discovering your identity, purpose, and faith. Just like the young shepherd boy David who faced a giant named Goliath, know that you possess incredible potential within you. You may feel small and insignificant, but remember that it was David's courage, faith, and trust in God that enabled him to defeat the seemingly unbeatable.
